Get to know us!

Who are we?

We are a group of professionals located in the Dominican Republic with the mission of offering the best health care to our veterans.

How do we do it?

We integrate multiple health services under a welcoming and heartwarming environment to satisfy all of our veteran’s needs.

This is What We Offer You

Physical Therapy

Our physical therapist provides personalized therapy and exercises to the need of each veteran based on their diagnosis, providing prompt relief and functional recovery. 

Health Care

We have doctors always ready to help our veterans, providing diagnosis, treatment and follow-ups to ensure the well-being of our veterans at all times.

Massages

Our masseuse has countless years of experience, providing massages that relax the musculoskeletal system, venting stress out of the body and helping our veterans empty their minds.

Psychiatry

Our psychiatrist tends to each of our veterans privately and personalized, adjusting therapies and follow-ups to their needs. We know how difficult it’s to not feel in the right mood, we are here to find a solution and make you feel the best you’ve ever been!

Psychology

Our psychologist will always be at your disposal to listen to your problems or any other issue, also working hand in hand with our psychiatrist, doctors, physical therapist and masseuse to ensure the integral well being of our veterans.
